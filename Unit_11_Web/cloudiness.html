As expected the weather becomes significativelly warmer as one aproaches the equator, (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere trends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemispehre. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
